💻 Software Development

Automated Code Review with Epistemic Awareness

The Challenge:
Traditional static analysis tools flag hundreds of issues, leading to alert fatigue. Developers ignore warnings because most are false positives, but real bugs get missed in the noise.

Empirica Solution:
Epistemic code review that quantifies confidence and prioritizes findings based on actual risk. The AI knows when it's certain vs. guessing.

Key Benefits:

  • Confidence-based filtering to prioritize genuine issues over false positives

  • Prioritized remediation focusing on high-confidence, high-impact issues

  • Uncertainty tracking for areas requiring additional investigation

  • Continuous learning through calibration: track which issues were actually bugs vs false alarms

🔒 Security Analysis

Vulnerability Assessment with Epistemic Prioritization

The Challenge:
Security scanners produce overwhelming numbers of alerts. Teams waste time investigating low-risk issues while critical vulnerabilities go unnoticed.

Empirica Solution:
Epistemic vulnerability assessment that prioritizes based on actual risk and evidence. The system explicitly tracks what it knows vs. what it's guessing about each finding.

Key Benefits:

  • Evidence-based prioritization of security findings

  • Reduced false negatives through systematic uncertainty tracking

  • Risk quantification with measured confidence

  • Investigation guidance for uncertain findings

🔬 Research & Analysis

Literature Review with Epistemic Validation

The Challenge:
Researchers often become overconfident about source reliability or miss important knowledge gaps. Confirmation bias leads to incomplete analysis.

Empirica Solution:
Systematic literature review with uncertainty tracking and source reliability assessment. The AI explicitly tracks what it knows, what it's uncertain about, and where gaps exist.

Key Benefits:

  • Systematic uncertainty tracking prevents overconfident conclusions

  • Source reliability scoring based on epistemic assessment

  • Bias detection through drift monitoring of reasoning patterns

  • Gap identification highlighting areas needing additional research

🎯 Decision Support Systems

Strategic Planning with Epistemic Risk Assessment

The Challenge:
Strategic decisions are often made with hidden overconfidence. Leaders don't know what they don't know, leading to poor outcomes and surprised stakeholders.

Empirica Solution:
Decision support that explicitly quantifies uncertainty and risks. The system measures confidence across all decision factors and highlights blind spots.

Key Benefits:

  • Explicit uncertainty quantification for each decision factor

  • Risk-aware recommendations based on epistemic state

  • Blind spot identification through systematic assessment

  • Confidence calibration to improve future decisions

🤖 Multi-AI Collaboration

Collaborative Reasoning with Shared Epistemic State

The Challenge:
Multiple AI systems working together often have conflicting assessments and no way to resolve disagreements. Manual coordination is slow and error-prone.

Empirica Solution:
Multi-AI system with shared epistemic state and collaborative decision-making. AIs can see each other's confidence levels and uncertainty, enabling intelligent coordination.

Key Benefits:

  • Shared belief repositories across AI systems

  • Conflict resolution based on epistemic confidence

  • Collective intelligence from multiple perspectives

  • Automated handoffs with ~400 token summaries

Getting Started with Your Use Case

Common Success Factors

When implementing Empirica in your domain, these factors drive success:

  1. Start with High-Impact Use Cases
    Focus on decisions with significant consequences where overconfidence is costly.

  2. Calibrate Domain-Specific Thresholds
    Adjust confidence thresholds for your industry. Security analysis needs higher confidence than research exploration.

  3. Invest in Investigation Strategies
    Develop domain-specific knowledge gathering approaches. Code analysis needs different strategies than literature review.

  4. Monitor Calibration Quality
    Track prediction accuracy over time. Are your confidence assessments matching reality?

  5. Iterate Based on Feedback
    Refine based on user experience and outcomes. Empirica gets better as it learns your domain.
